Fun Facts

Here are a couple fun facts about the Staplees and Covell Sawmill

  1. It was the first steam powerd sawmill in Whitehall Michigan
  2. It was built by Charles Mears
  3. It was created in 1856
  4. They could turn 1,400 logs into 60,000 board feet of lumber during an 11-hour shift.
